About The Product

Highly effective yet gentle serum for hyperpigmentation, sun-induced dullness and uneven skin tone. The 2% Kojic Acid and Alpha Arbutin serum penetrates deep into the skin, disperses excess melanin to fade dark spots and hyperpigmentation for clearer, even-toned skin. With antioxidative benefits, Kojic Acid diminishes existing spots while preventing the formation of new ones by fighting free radicals’ damage. Alpha Arbutin evens out discolouration caused by acne, sun damage and post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation. Over time, this potent serum makes dull, pigmented skin visibly clearer and even-toned, by inhibiting the production of excess melanin, exfoliating dead skin cells and boosting radiance.
